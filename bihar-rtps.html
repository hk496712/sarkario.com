Document Required ?

  • Aadhar card
  • mobile no
  • Address proof
  • Khatihan

How to apply online for caste, income, domicile certificate ?

  • Click on Apply online link
  • click on 'from where you want to receive the cerificate block/ bihar bhawan, new delhi.
  • Enter your name, aadhar no. etc
  • choose which certificate you want to online i.e caste / income/ domicile/ tatkal caste etc.
  • enter mobile no.
  • click on next button
  • you get verification code
  • tick check box and enter verification code
  • take a Print out and final submit.
