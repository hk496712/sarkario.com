How can you apply for RTI ?

  • click on sign up/ Registration
  • User ID generated
  • After sign in you have go to 'submit Request' which is given below
  • Read Guidelines
  • check the check box and submit
  • fill all details , Department, and your question/ query (max 3000 words)
  • verify security code
  • click on submit
  • submit payment
  • congrats you fill rti successfully.

How can you check your RTI status ?

  • Check below link 'view status'
  • fill registration no. , email , security code and click on submit.
  • Congrats now you can see your RTI status.
