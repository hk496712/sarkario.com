About Post:PAN card -permanent account number(PAN) is issued by indian income tax Department. PAN card id mandatory for opening a new bank account, a new landline telephone connection/a mobile phone connection,purchase foreign currency ,bank deposit more than 50,000 ruppess , Income tax returns form filling, Tax deduction at sources etc. and also any other communication with the income tax department .

Document Required for Pan card online

  • Aadhar Card
  • Voter id
  • Driving License
  • Passport
  • Ration Card
  • Arm’s License
  • Pensioner Card
  • Photo id card (central/state)
  • any other Valid Document
  • Download-Document acceptable as Proof of Identity

How to apply for pan card online

  • Open Application form
  • Click in Apply for new Pan card (form 49A)
  • choose your category like individual,Government,company,firm etc.
  • Enter your Basic Details like your name, father name, date of birth, etc.
  • click on submit button
  • pay Fee
  • print out your Acknowledgement page for future.
