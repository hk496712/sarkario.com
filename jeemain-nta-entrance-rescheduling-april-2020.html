Rescheduling of JOINT ENTRANCE EXAMINATION JEE (Main) April -2020 Examination

date:22.05.2020

The JOINT ENTRANCE EXAMINATION JEE (Main) April -2020 Examination which was to be conducted on 5th,7th to 9th and 11th April 2020 had to be postponed due to the extraordinary circumstances arising from COVID-19 pandemic. A Public Notice dated 31.03.2020 was issued in this regard.

The rescheduled exam date will be 18.07.2020 to 23.07.2020.

Admit card downloding will be available 15 days before.

The candidates and their parents are advised to keep visiting website for latest updates
